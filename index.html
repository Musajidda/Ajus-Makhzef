About

Who we are

Ajus Makkhdzef Limited: A Brief History Founded in 2024 in Nigeria, Ajus Makkhdzef Limited quickly established itself as a dynamic force in the global trade industry.

  • Global: Connecting markets worldwide with seamless trade solutions.
  • Innovation: Evolving with cutting-edge strategies for global commerce.
  • Reliability: Trusted for consistent, timely, and quality service.

Ajus Makkhdzef Limited specializes in both export and import services and was born out of a vision to connect Nigeria with markets around the world, facilitating the flow of goods and resources across borders. With a commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, Ajus Makkhdzef Limited has positioned itself as a trusted partner in the international trade sector, helping businesses navigate the complexities of global commerce and fostering economic growth both locally and globally.

Product

What we have

  • All

Kola Nut

This is the seed of the kola tree, native to tropical Africa. It's historically important in West African culture for social ceremonies, medicinal uses, and as a stimulant because it contains caffeine.

Hibiscus

Hibiscus is a vibrant tropical flower used in teas and drinks, known for its tangy flavor and health benefits, including antioxidants and potential blood pressure reduction.

Cashew Nut

Cashew nuts are kidney-shaped seeds from the cashew tree, native to Brazil but widely cultivated in tropical regions. They are a popular snack, rich in healthy fats, protein, and essential nutrients like magnesium and copper.

Ginger

Ginger is a spicy root used as both a spice and a medicinal herb. Native to Southeast Asia, it is known for its strong flavor and anti-inflammatory properties, often used to aid digestion and reduce nausea.

Wheat

Wheat is a staple cereal grain widely grown around the world. It is a key ingredient in bread, pasta, and other food products, providing essential nutrients like carbohydrates, fiber, and vitamins. It is a major source of energy in many diets globally.

Groundnut

Groundnut, also known as peanut, is a legume rich in protein, healthy fats, and essential nutrients. It is widely consumed as a snack, used in cooking oils, and processed into products like peanut butter.

Palm Nut

Palm nuts come from the oil palm tree and are primarily used to produce palm oil and palm kernel oil. These oils are widely used in cooking, cosmetics, and industrial applications.

Soybeans

Soybeans are a versatile legume used worldwide for food, animal feed, and industrial products. They are rich in protein and oil, making them a staple in products like tofu, soy milk, and soy sauce.

Charcoal

Charcoal is a lightweight, black carbon residue produced by heating wood or other organic materials in the absence of oxygen. It is commonly used as a fuel for cooking, heating, and industrial purposes, and is valued for its ability to burn at high temperatures with minimal smoke.
